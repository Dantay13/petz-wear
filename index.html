Petz-Wear was founded in 2021 by two young entrepreneurs who love animals. Having a few dogs, a couple of cats, a pair of bunnies, and a few birds, they decided why not make our pets fashionable. From this initiative, Petz-Wear was established in the beautiful Cayman Islands.

Petz-Wear vision is to provide fashionable outfits for all categories of pets with no discrimination. Petz-wear plans to offer customizable, unique pieces, in which each pet can express its unique personality. It can be as minimal as a doggie bandana, as flashy as branded outfits, and as luxurious as pieces of jewelry. Petz-Wear will be the one-stop e-commerce site to roam, shop and pay for all your pets' fashion needs.
